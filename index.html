Who We Are

We are two college kids who decided to start a clothing brand to make streetwear fashion avalible to everyone. All of our pieces are made with sustainable materials making good fashion affordable

This project is also our first website we have built. We are Computer Science students with a desire to make ourselves stand out from the crowd-- not only with our fashion--but with our software development as well.
